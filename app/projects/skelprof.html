Skeletome Knowledge Base

What is it?

  • focused on rare genetic disorders affecting human skeleton "bone dysplasias"
  • research collaboration between University of Queensland (School of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering) and Royal Brisbane and Women's Hospital (RBWH)
  • funded by arc linkage (government grant)
  • Skeletome

The need

  • let medical professionals in the field share their knowledge
  • bring together the community spread throughout the world in one place
  • get the knowledge out, available and searchable - get it off email
  • let them share knowledge that might not yet be journal worthy
  • let them discuss new knowledge
